  • Then, you'll have to pay your annual IHSA membership fee (don't worry, this will be reimbursed!).

Van Certification

For every lesson, show, or other off-site team outing, there MUST be at least one certified driver per van. Every year, many lessons need to be cancelled due to lack of certified drivers. Therefore, it's extremely important that everyone who is eligible to become van certified does so.

You are eligible to become driver certified if:

  • You have had your license for at least 2 years.
  • You do not have an at-fault accident on your record.

If you are eligible, then you can sign up to become driver certified in two steps:

  • First, you must complete the Defensive Driving Course.
  • Secondly, you must meet with Joyce Oakes to go over all of the rules.

Each of these steps takes only ~1 hour each, and it's absolutely essential to the operation of this team for us to have enough certified drivers, so please make sure to become certified if you're eligible!

Scholarships

Of course, horseback riding has never been a cheap sport. Thankfully, there are a few scholarships which members of our team can take advantage of.

  • Student Activities Assistance Fund (SAAF) - SAAF is a scholarship fund through Northwestern which is allocated to students who participate in various student clubs and organizations. Students can receive up to $600 per year through SAAF. The application can be found here, and the application deadline is rolling.
  • Quarterly Scholarship (Show Team Members Only) - This is a team-funded scholarship which we offer to two Show Team members each quarter who demonstrate both devotion to the team and financial need. This scholarship is for $200 per quarter and may be used for any team-related expense. To be eligible, you must also apply for SAAF. The application can be found here and is due the Sunday of Week 1 at 11:59 PM.
