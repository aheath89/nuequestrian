The Northwestern University Equestrian Team is a club-level hunt seat equitation team that competes on the Intercollegiate Horse Shows Association circuit. Our team also offers weekly lessons, in addition to our competitions.

Our team was founded in 2008, and we currently train with Val Buesching, owner and head trainer at On Course Riding Academy, located at Birch Hill Farm in Wauconda, IL.
