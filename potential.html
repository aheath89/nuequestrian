Hello! Thank you for your interest in our team! Here you can find all the information you'll need to know about how to officially join our team!

To officially join the equestrian team, there's a few key things you'll need to do!

Registering

  • Firstly, if you're looking to join after the club fair, you should send us an email at nuequestrian@gmail.com so that we can add you to our email list! If the club fair hasn't happened yet, you can skip this step - we'll add you to our email list at the club fair!
  • Next up, you MUST register with the Equestrian Team on IMLeagues before you'll be able to attend any lessons or competitions. You can find instructions for how to join our team on IMLeagues here!
  • Next you'll want to fill out our Team Registration Form!
  • If you're a new member, we ask that you review our Horse Safety PowerPoint and complete a short quiz.

    • IHSA

  • Lastly, if you're planning on competing in the IHSA with our team, then you'll need to create a new Individual Membership on the IHSA website, located here. Once you've created your membership, you must complete all of the "Things to Do" action items located on the right side of your screen.
    • Pay the IHSA membership fee (don't worry, this will be reimbursed!
    • Complete the Rider Placement Form - this will help us determine which IHSA division you should compete in!

And that's it! Once you've completed these steps, you'll be on your way to being an official member of the Northwestern Equestrian Team!
